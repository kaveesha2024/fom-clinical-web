üõëWhy it matters

1Insulin resistance and chronic diseases

Insulin resistance is a condition where the body's cells become less responsive to insulin, leading to higher blood sugar levels. Over time, this can contribute to type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and metabolic syndrome, increasing the risk of serious health complications.

2Impact of unhealthy diets & sedentary lifestyles

Poor dietary choices, such as consuming processed foods and sugary drinks, combined with a lack of physical activity, can significantly raise the risk of non-communicable diseases (NCDs) like obesity, hypertension, and cardiovascular diseases. Adopting a healthier lifestyle can help prevent these risks

3Early lifestyle changes can prevent and even reverse chronic diseases.

Adopting healthier habits early can halt or even reverse the progression of chronic conditions, enhancing quality of life and reducing long-term healthcare costs.
